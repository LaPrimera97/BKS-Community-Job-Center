Our Purpose

"Built to ensure that local residents are first in line — fairly, verifiably, and on record."

BKS Community Job Center was created to address a persistent gap in South Africa's mining sector: community members living adjacent to mines are often last to benefit from the employment those mines generate — despite Social and Labour Plan obligations that require otherwise.

Our platform gives mines a verifiable, DMRE-auditable system for community recruitment, and gives job seekers a simple way to register, apply, and track their applications.

We don't just facilitate employment. We create the audit trail that protects mines at Section 93 review, and the transparency that protects communities from being overlooked.

How it works

Three steps — from community member to verified applicant. Accessible on any device.

Step 01

Register

Create your community job seeker profile. Submit your ID, skills, and qualifications securely through our POPIA-compliant portal on any smartphone.

Step 02

Verify

Your location is confirmed via GPS to establish community eligibility, creating an auditable record for SLP compliance — transparent and tamper-proof.

Step 03

Apply

Browse open positions, submit applications, and track your status in real time — all through a single interface built for your community.

Platform Capabilities

Built for compliance.
Designed for people.

Community Job Seeker Portal

Mobile-responsive registration and application system — accessible to any community resident with a basic smartphone, in English or Sepedi.

Secure Admin Dashboard

Real-time analytics, applicant management, job posting controls, and SHA-256 encrypted records for mine administrators and HR teams.

SLP Tracking & Reporting

Track actuals against Regulation 46 targets, flag Section 93 risk automatically, and generate DMRE-formatted annual reports ready for submission.

GPS Location Verification

Haversine-based distance check confirms community eligibility per application — creating a verifiable, auditable trail for every submission.

Bilingual Interface

Full English and Sepedi language support across all user-facing screens — ensuring genuine accessibility for mine host communities across South Africa's mining regions.

Automated Document Management

Cloud-based record storage, automated email notifications, base64 CV uploads, and Google Drive integration — end-to-end workflow automation.
