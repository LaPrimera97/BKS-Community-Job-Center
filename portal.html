ABOUT US

Built for local communities.
Powered by local leadership.

BKS Community Job Center connects local families to mine employment opportunities — fairly, transparently, and on merit.

We work alongside traditional leadership and local committees — not around them — bringing digital efficiency to a process that directly impacts livelihoods across our communities.

Regulatory & Legislative Alignment

The BKS Portal is fully aligned with the Mineral and Petroleum Resources Development Act (MPRDA), Act No. 28 of 2002, and supports the Department of Mineral Resources and Energy (DMRE) regulatory framework — including Social and Labour Plan (SLP) obligations.

Community-Led Governance

We are committed to meaningful stakeholder engagement — respecting traditional leadership, their structures, relevant communities, and all interested and affected parties in the candidate selection process.
